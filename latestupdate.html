About Himalay Darshan

“Founded on September 30, 2025, Himalay Darshan Social Service Organization works to promote Indian culture, human values, health, education, and the holistic development of underprivileged women and children, while also focusing on environmental and soil conservation, disaster awareness, preparedness, and relief efforts. Through positive initiatives and meaningful programs, it inspires action, drives change, addresses social challenges, and advances human welfare.”
